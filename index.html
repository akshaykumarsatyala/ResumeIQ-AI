FAQ

Questions that typically come up before launch.

How does the ATS score work?

It compares your resume structure, keywords, and role alignment against an example job requirement profile and returns a readable match score.

Can I track multiple internships?

Yes. The tracker is designed as a pipeline with applied, interview, selected, rejected, and wishlist states.

Is there interview prep built in?

The preview shows mock interview scoring, question banks, and AI feedback summaries in one place.

Does it support mobile usage?

Yes. Mobile and tablet previews are included below to validate the responsive layout before build-out.
