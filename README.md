# 🚀 ResumeIQ AI

> Analyze • Optimize • Get Hired

ResumeIQ AI is a modern AI-powered Resume Analyzer & ATS Checker designed to help students, freshers, and job seekers improve their resumes, understand ATS compatibility, identify missing skills, and prepare for internship opportunities.
